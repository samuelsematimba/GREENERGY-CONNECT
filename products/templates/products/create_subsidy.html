How Subsidies Work
1. Create
Define the subsidy with its name, code, discount amount or percentage, and which products and outlets it applies to.
2. At Point of Sale
When an agent makes a sale at an eligible outlet, active subsidies for the selected products will appear as a dropdown on the sale form.
3. Discount Applied
Selecting a subsidy automatically adjusts the sale total. The discount amount is recorded against the sale for reconciliation and reporting.
Discount Types
Fixed Amount
A set amount is deducted from the sale total. E.g. if the product is 200,000 and subsidy is 50,000 fixed, customer pays 150,000.
Percentage
A percentage is deducted. E.g. 20% off a 200,000 product = 40,000 discount, customer pays 160,000.
